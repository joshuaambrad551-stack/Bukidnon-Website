RR Family Spring Resort

The Refreshing Heart of Maramag

Maramag, Bukidnon 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
The Experience

Bukidnon's Inland Oasis

RR Family Spring Resort is one of the most popular water destinations in the province. Known for its crystal-clear, flowing spring waters, it offers a "beach-like" relaxation experience in the middle of landlocked Bukidnon.

With vast Olympic-sized pools and smaller natural springs surrounded by tropical cottages, it is the perfect destination for family reunions, weekend getaways, and beating the tropical heat.

Visitor Information
  • Entrance Fee: ₱50.00 (Adults) / ₱30.00 (Kids)
  • Cottages: Starting at ₱300.00
  • Parking: Large onsite parking area available
Amenities

What Awaits You

Natural Springs

Enjoy chemical-free swimming in pools fed by continuous natural spring water flowing from the mountains.

Cottage Life

Spacious open-air huts and private gazebos perfect for boodle fights, BBQ, and large family picnics.

Night Swimming

Stay late and enjoy the beautifully lit pools and the refreshing cool evening breeze of Maramag.

Ready for a Dip?

Visit Maramag today and experience the freshest waters in the province.
