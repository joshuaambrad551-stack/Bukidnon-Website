Bukidnon at a Glance

Geography

Total Land Area: 1,049,859 hectares. The 4th largest province in the Philippines.

Demographics

Population: ~1.5 Million. A melting pot of 7 indigenous tribes and settlers.

Climate

Average Temperature: 18°C – 28°C. Cool and misty year-round.
